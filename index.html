The game of Pigs is a dice game with simple rules. Here are the basic rules of the game:

  • The game is played with two players taking turns. On each turn, a player rolls a dice.
  • The player accumulates points based on the sum of the dice rolls.
    • If the player rolls a 1 on the dice, their turn ends, and they lose all points accumulated in that turn. - It's called "pigging out."
  • If the player rolls any other number, they can choose to roll again and add to their current points, or they can end their turn and bank their accumulated points.
  • The player can continue rolling as long as they avoid rolling a 1 or choose to stop and bank their points.
  • The first player to reach a winning point total (50) wins the game.
